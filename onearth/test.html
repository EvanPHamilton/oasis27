About us

The onEarth Foundation, established in 2019, provides alternative ecological education by bridging art, technology, and science. Its mission is to share environmental awareness and embrace equity of all living beings by creating spaces for entanglement, co-creation, deep adaptation and wellbeing.

Purpose and Values

The onEarth Foundation is committed to re-establishing symbiotic relationships between humans and Planet Earth. Through imagination, play and interdisciplinary collaboration it aims to embrace environmental, social and personal response-ability, trust and sustainable futures. Our mission:

  • Embrace solidarity and diversity
  • Share knowledge, skills and new possibilities of collaboration
  • Integrate artistic and ecological circles

OASIS 2027 (OA27)

OASIS 2027 (OA27) is a research residency programme leading to the creation of an OA27 Festival of Strategies for Climate Emergency launching in October 2022. The seed topic of the OA27 programm is desertification: the processes of fertile lands becoming increasingly arid, mostly due to human activities. According to scientific reports, the tangible effects of desertification will affect us as soon as 2027
