About us

The onEarth Foundation, established in 2019, provides alternative ecological education by bridging art, technology, and science. Its mission is to share environmental awareness and embrace equity of all living beings by creating spaces for entanglement, co-creation, deep adaptation and wellbeing.

Purpose and Values
